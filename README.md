# ConnConnect
Turn you iPad into an excellent kiosk to sign-up fans or colleages to your mailing list  with style, diplaying your unique images and story.

## Features
* Creates a slide show to display any number of images that reflect who you are and your products.
* Add a custom banner displaying your name and even yur custom Logo.
* 'Sign Up' button and banner backgrounds can be in one of three modes. (Glass, Frosted, or Color Gradient).
*  Generous, but limited choice of fonts and sizes for both the banner and button text.
*  Banner size is adjustable to fit the text.
